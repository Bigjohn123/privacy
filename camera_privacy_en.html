OnCamera - Privacy Policy Effective Date: December 19, 2025 1. Introduction OnCamera is a dual-lens camera app that supports front & rear camera simultaneous recording, split-screen, picture-in-picture, real-time filters, and beauty effects. We are committed to protecting user privacy. 2. Data Collection & Usage OnCamera does NOT collect, upload, or share your personal data: All videos, photos, and recordings are stored locally on your device only No facial data, video content, or personal information is sent to cloud servers No unnecessary data (location, contacts, messages) is collected 3. Permissions Usage OnCamera requests only essential permissions for core functions: Camera: For front & rear camera preview and recording Microphone: For audio capture during video recording Photo Library: For saving videos and photos to your device album Storage: For local video file processing All permissions are optional and can be disabled in system settings at any time. 4. Data Storage & Security All media files are saved locally to your device album No cloud storage or remote backup No data transmission to third parties All data is removed when the app is uninstalled 5. Third-Party Services OnCamera does NOT include ads, trackers, or analytics SDKs. No user data is shared with third parties. 6. User Rights You may: Capture, edit, delete, or export videos and photos Enable or disable app permissions at any time Uninstall the app to remove all local data 7.
