Privacy Statement (OxHorse Secretary) Core Privacy Commitments 1. Local-First Data Storage OxHorse Secretary adheres to a "Private Dungeon Storage" principle, leveraging Apple's latest SwiftData framework to ensure all schedule data (including task names, deadlines, recurrence settings, and category information) is stored exclusively on the owner's (user's) local device. No scheduling data is transmitted to or stored on external cloud servers without explicit user consent. 2. Image Privacy Protection The "Owner's Collection" feature allows users to select cover images from their photo albums for countdown tasks. We solemnly commit that all selected cover images are stored locally only on the device. These images will never be uploaded to cloud servers, shared with third parties, or used for any commercial or analytical purposes—your precious photos remain your exclusive secret. 3. Notification & Data Security All reminder notifications are delivered via the device's native local push system. We do not collect, track, or analyze user behavior related to notification interactions (e.g., whether a reminder is viewed or clicked). No personal data associated with notifications (such as reminder times, content, or recipient information) is shared with external entities. 4. Future Feature Privacy (Roadmap) Multi-Device Sync (iCloud): If the future iCloud sync feature is enabled by the user, data synchronization will be conducted solely through Apple's official iCloud service. We will not access or store any synced data; all data transmission and storage comply with Apple's iCloud privacy standards. Customization Features: Any user-defined category names, icons, or other personalized settings will remain stored locally by default, with sync optional and fully controlled by the user. 5. No Unauthorized Data Collection OxHorse Secretary does not collect any non-essential personal information (such as device identifiers, location data, browsing history, or usage analytics) without the user's explicit permission. We will never sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties for commercial gain. Your privacy is our top priority. OxHorse Secretary pledges to guard your personal data with the utmost loyalty—what you wish to keep confidential will remain confidential, forever locked within the "private dungeon" of your own device.
